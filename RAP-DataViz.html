RAP Data Visualization

As part of my role in Data Applications for the Rangeland Analysis Platform (RAP), I've developed data visualizations, maps, and animations to depict spatiotemporal data. These visualizations have ultimately been used in communications and informational products for partner groups and public audiences.

  • Project lead Eric Jensen
  • Collaborators None
  • Websitehttps://rangelands.app/production-explorer/
  • Completed September 2021

Below are a collection of data visualizations that I have produced using Rangeland Analysis Platform data for communications and outreach to the public and partner groups.
